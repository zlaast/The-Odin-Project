Slinky Sliders

Ingredients

  • 1 Slinky
  • 100g of salted butter
  • 100g of flour
  • 250ml of vegetable stock
  • A tomato
  • Some lettuce
  • 1 bread bun

Steps

  1. Stretch out your slinky, so it is nice and loose
  2. In a ripping hot pan, add some oil and cook your slinky
  3. Add salt and seasoning to your slinky while it's cooking
  4. After cooking your slinky, in the same pan, add the butter. Let it melt
  5. Add your flour and stir to make a rue
  6. When the rue has thickened, pour in the vegetable stock and let thicken to become a sauce
  7. Toast your bread
  8. Assemble your sandwich
    1. Place your bun bottom and add some sauce on it
    2. Then add a slice of tomato
    3. Add some lettuce
    4. Add your cooked slinky
    5. Add some more lettuce
    6. Add another slice of tomato
    7. Add some more sauce
    8. Finish your sandwich by placing the bun top on top
