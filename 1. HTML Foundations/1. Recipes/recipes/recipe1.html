Snuggledorfs in a Blanket

Ingredients

  • 3kg of Snuggledorfs
  • 1kg of butter
  • 10 sheets of puff pastry

Steps

  1. Cut the Snuggledorfs in 10cm lengths
  2. Cook the Snuggledorfs in butter
  3. Cut the puff pastry to 8cm x 6cm strips
  4. Wrap the cooked Snuggledorfs in the puff pastry
  5. Bake the wrapped Snuggledorfs at 350F
