Two Peas in a Pod

Ingredients

  • 100 Snap Peas, still in their pod
  • 1L Water
  • 30g Salt
  • 1L Cold Water

Steps

  1. Bring the 1L of water to a boil
  2. Add the salt to the water
  3. Put the snap peas into the boiling water and let cook for about 1 minute
  4. Take out the peas and immediately dunk in the 1L of cold water
